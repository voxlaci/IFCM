Why IFCM exists

Born to connect
a world that sings.

In 1982, five major choral organisations came together in Namur, Belgium, with one purpose: make communication and exchange possible across borders.

Since then, IFCM has connected musicians through world symposia, the World Youth Choir, World Choral Day, publications, mentoring, databases and international cooperation. Its reason remains urgent: when people sing together, differences of culture, language and belief can become shared creation.
