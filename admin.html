What activates
real publishing?

1
IFCM administrator login

Only authorised editors can approve, schedule or retry posts.

2
Meta app and permissions

A Page access token and approved Facebook/Instagram publishing permissions.

3
Professional Instagram account

The IFCM Instagram professional account must be correctly connected to its Facebook Page.

4
Secure server workflow

Store tokens outside the browser, publish through server functions and keep an audit log.

5
Retries and moderation

Failed posts remain visible to administrators and can be safely retried.

This prototype simulates publishing locally. It never requests or stores Facebook, Instagram or payment credentials.
